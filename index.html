GO-GO Travels

Go,Go travel the World!

Go-Go Travels, an organisation working in the field of Eco-tourism is a recognized name in the domain of 'Wildlife, Adventure and Trekking Holidays'. In the past eighteen years, Foliage Outdoors has emerged as the only avenue, offering seekers a diverse mix of Wildlife, Adventure, Trekking and Nature holidays. We have been conducting trips to international destinations, right from Arctic to Antarctica; encompassing African Safaris to Kenya, Tanzania, Botswana, Zimbabwe, Namibia and South Africa; Birding Tours to Bhutan, and Treks to Everest and Annapurna Base Camp in Nepal.

Go-Go Travels is a professionally managed organisation, having a team of experts, well versed in all the aspects of nature. Our resource persons, have expertise in various aspects of Natural History like mammals, birds, reptiles, butterflies, and insects. Our team dwellers, not only have a keen eye for Flora and Fauna but they also have a unique ability to impart information to special interest groups as well as beginners.
